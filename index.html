🛬 For families relocating from BC: French Immersion entry ages differ by board.
  • Peel (PDSB/DPCDSB) — Mississauga: FI entry is Grade 1, apply in January. Lottery selection; siblings no priority.
  • Toronto (TDSB/TCDSB) — Etobicoke: Early FI entry is Senior Kindergarten (SK) / JK, apply in early–late November. On-time applicants guaranteed a program spot (not a specific school). Middle FI Gr 4 entry applies in January.
  • French-language schools (Viamonde, MonAvenir) require Section 23 Charter eligibility — one parent educated in French in Canada, or an eligible sibling. Ideal if a parent is already francophone.
⚠️ Catchments, entry grades, and program availability change annually. Verify your home address via PDSB Find My School, TDSB Find Your School, DPCDSB, and TCDSB before making any enrolment decisions.
